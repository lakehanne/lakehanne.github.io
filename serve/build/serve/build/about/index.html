About



I am currently a PhD student at the University of Texas at Dallas, studying control systems (mostly nonlinear dynamical systems). My advisor is Nick Gans.

Prior to this, I had my Master of Science in Engineering at the University of Sheffield, Sheffield, England where I majored in control systems under the advisement of Tony J. Dodd. I had my bachelors with a double major in Physics and Electronics from Adekunle Ajasin University in Nigeria. My senior thesis (Single-Fractional Parentage Coefficients in the sd-Shell Nuclei) was advised by Ademola Amusa.

My research interests include adaptive system identification for control, computer vision, stability in switched systems and controls, and machine learning.

My academic webpage has much more details on my research.
